🛠️ Communication Breakdown Simulation

(Forum or worksheet activity)

  • Employees will be presented with a simple workplace scenario where communication failed (e.g., email misinterpretation, tone confusion, unclear instructions).
  • Task: Identify what went wrong in the scenario and rewrite a better version of the message or describe how they would improve the communication in person.

📸 Slack Scenario:

Chris Mitchell (6:19 PM): The power went out for a few minutes just now and I lost everything I’d been working on for the past 3 hours!!!

Jamie Smith (6:33 PM): 🫣🧠🧯🫠🔋📉😵‍💫🪫☠️

Chris Mitchell (6:36 PM): ? Please use words..

✍️ Instruction: After reviewing the scenario above, reflect on what went wrong and how it could have been communicated more clearly.
