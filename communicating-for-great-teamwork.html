Communicating for Great Teamwork

PROJECT CORE - Soft Skills Short Course - Research

Status: Completed

To help new employees build strong communication habits that support aligned, inclusive, and future-ready teams.

Week 1: What Makes a Great Team?

Objective: Understand how communication contributes to team alignment and shared goals.

Video: What Makes a Team Great?

Quiz: Q&As

Main Activity: Reflect on a team you've been part of (3 points: one thing done well, one breakdown, one thing you'd do differently).

Week 2: Being an Active Communicator in Teams

Objective: Learn to communicate in ways that build trust, clarify direction, and move the team forward.

Video: 4 Keys To Effective Team Communication

Reading: Fostering Trust Within Teams (Harvard Business Review)

Quiz: Q&As

Main Activity: Submit a short script or voice note showing how you'd clarify direction while maintaining trust.

Week 3: Understanding People: Personalities, Preferences & Strengths

Objective: Recognize personality types and communication styles that impact team collaboration.

Video: Understanding Team Personality Types and Team Dynamics

Reading: Whatâ€™s Your DISC Personality Type? (Quiz)

Quiz: Q&As

Main Activity: DISC reflection: your type + what it means for your team communication.

Week 4: Handling Common Team Communication Challenges

Objective: Apply communication skills to navigate small conflicts, misunderstandings, or team friction.

Video: Team Leadership: Handling Team Problems

Quiz: Q&As

Main Activity: Choose a scenario and write your supportive, clear, respectful response.

Week 5: Supporting Team Growth & Development Conversations

Objective: Understand how day-to-day communication supports development, learning, and planning for future needs.

Video: Encouraging Your Team To Grow Their Skills

Reading: Feedback Isn't Improving (Gallup)

Quiz: Chapter quiz (Week 1-3 content + reading)

Week 6: Putting It All Together: Communication That Builds Teams

Objective: Reflect on what you've learned and apply it to a real or hypothetical team situation.

Video: Fostering Effective Team Collaboration and Communication

Main Activity: Final output: Team Communication Plan (purpose, roles, norms, conflict prevention, growth check-ins, sample touchpoint).
