Week 1: Who Are Our Stakeholders, and Why Do They Matter?

Objective: Understand the landscape of external stakeholders (e.g. regulators, partners, communities, suppliers).

Video: Who Are External Stakeholders?; Internal vs External Stakeholders in Business

Reading: What Are Stakeholders? Definition, Types, and Examples

Quiz: Match stakeholders to influence types; basic terminology Q&As

Main Activity: Pick one external stakeholder group, answer key questions (importance, impact, positive relationship actions).

Other Handovers: Stakeholder Mapping Template

Week 2: How to Build Positive Influence with Stakeholders

Objective: Learn how to ethically shape influence through empathy, alignment, and timing.

Video: Influence Isn’t Pressure — It’s Connection; How to Influence Your Stakeholders (Part 1)

Week 3: Communicating Vision & Values to the Outside World

Objective: Learn how to communicate the company’s mission, strategy, and values to external partners.

Video: How to Speak for the Organization; What Is Stakeholder Management?; How to Communicate and Influence Stakeholders

Main Activity: Draft external communication piece sharing mission/values, external-fit, team reinforcement.

Week 4: Representing the Company in External Spaces

Objective: Learn how to engage new stakeholders and reinforce relationships.

Video: What is Stakeholder Engagement?; Engaging Stakeholders

Reading: Stakeholder Engagement: What It Means & Why It Matters

Main Activity: Draft intro + one relationship-building comment for an external meeting simulation.

Week 5: Stakeholder Engagement in Complex Conversations

Objective: Build confidence in addressing sensitive or high-impact issues and align messaging.

Video: Our Voice in the World: Navigating Complexity; How to Manage Difficult Stakeholders; How to Talk to Stakeholders; Managing Stakeholders Expectations

Reading: Stakeholder Management Guide

Quiz: Situational choices + chapter quiz

Main Activity: Draft talking note on sensitive topic (acknowledge concerns, align position, trade-offs/next steps).

Week 6: Your Voice, Our Values

Objective: Reflect on your role in stakeholder engagement and create a practical plan or piece of communication.

Video: Engaging Stakeholders: Strategies for Success; What Is A Stakeholder Engagement Plan?; Reviewing the Stakeholder Engagement Plan

Main Activity: Create Stakeholder Engagement Plan: identify stakeholder, choose communication piece, trust-building actions.
