What is Project C.O.R.E?

Project CORE is a team-led initiative by HR-LOE designed to empower our organization and its members through self-paced online short courses.

It supports individual career growth by focusing on modern-day soft skills, encouraging learning by doing, and nurturing higher-order thinking.

Through interactive exercises and self-discovery tools, learners can actively shape their professional development journey at their own pace.

What we focus on

Self-Paced Learning

Access short online courses anytime, anywhere, at your own pace.

Higher-Order Thinking

Sharpen decision-making, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.

Career-Aligned Growth

Build relevant soft skills that support your professional journey.
