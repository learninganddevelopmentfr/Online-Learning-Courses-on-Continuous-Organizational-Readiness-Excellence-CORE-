Thriving Through Change

PROJECT CORE - Soft Skills Short Course - Research

Status: Completed

Reference: Change Management Toolkit

Week 1: Understanding the Impacts of Change

Objective: Recognize how change impacts people, risk, and productivity and why those factors matter.

Video: Understanding the Impact of Changes; Navigate and Embrace Change | Simon Sinek

Reading: Building Trust Through Effective Organizational Change Communication

Quiz: Q&As

Other Handovers: Impact Assessment PDF

Week 2: How Change Activities Work Across Teams and Regions

Objective: Understand the basic structure of effective change activities (communications, timelines, ownership, check-ins).

Video: What is Change Management?; 5 Steps in the Change Management Process

Week 3: Working With Support Functions to Enable Change

Objective: Understand how functions like HR, Finance, and Engineering support change and learn how anyone can coordinate with support functions (even without authority).

Video: What to do with support functions?; What's YOUR Role in Change Management?

Main Activity: Choose a support function; describe partnership + submit 2 collaboration steps.

Week 4: Adapting and Asking the Right Questions in Change

Objective: Recognize challenges in cross-functional or multi-region change and consider how to check alignment, verify if change is working, and make adjustments.

Video: How to Adapt to Change in the Workplace

Reading: Seven R’s Change Management Checklist

Week 5: Leading with Care During Change

Objective: Explore how transparency, trust, and collaboration shape successful change.

Video: How to Approach Change Management with Transparency; Why Transparency is Critical to Creating Trust

Reading: How to Approach Change Management with Transparency — Galen Emanuele

Quiz: Chapter quiz

Week 6: Your Role in Change

Objective: Reflect on your role in enabling, adapting to, or supporting change.

Video: How to Approach Change Management with Transparency; What's YOUR Role in Change Management?

Main Activity: Submit “My Role in Change” Plan: key role, actions, 3 practical steps going forward.
